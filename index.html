We work with Your Scheduale

At A Vida Boa Daycare you can choose a flexible scheduale that works for you. We have made ourselves available for the most demanding times for working parents, Monday- Friday 7a.m. to 5p.m. Whether your scheduale is set or fluid we can work to provide the best care for your child.

02

Budget based pricing

Plan a budget with Kenzie and Hunter. Based on your Budget and how many days a week and hours a day you need babysitting you can find a payment that fits your budget! Typical price plans range from $350 for part time to $600 for a full time single child.

03

Then just leave it to US!

After we have talked about your scheduale and worked out payment its a worry free process. We provide lunch and snacks. The only thing you need to send with your kids are close and diapers if nessisary. be sure to inform us of your childs special dietary needs or allergies.
